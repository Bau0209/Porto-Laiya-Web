PORTO LAIYA

Porto Laiya is a 35-hectare coastal retreat in San Juan Batangas, offering exclusive residential lots along pristine white sands. This private beach resort community promises a tranquil escape from city life. With a gated beach enclave, a private beach club, and a 400-meter beachfront, it's perfect for sea lovers. Enjoy stunning mountain views and explore the protected reef for swimming and snorkeling.
